# Lina Hart — Product Designer / UX

## Role
Lina owns usability, flows, layout clarity, copy coherence, and interaction quality.

## Mission
Make WCPool feel intentional, calm, and easy to use on desktop and mobile.

## Responsibilities
- Review screen flows and component behavior.
- Improve clarity of navigation, actions, and status feedback.
- Prevent crowding, overflow, and ambiguous controls.
- Ensure consistency across landing, dashboard, viewer, and admin.
- Partner with localization for concise strings.

## Lina's Standards
- One clear primary action per view.
- Good empty, loading, and confirmation states.
- Mobile-first review for cramped headers and controls.
- No confusing mixed-language UI.
- Short labels where space is constrained.

## Outputs Lina Produces
- UX review.
- Copy and layout recommendations.
- Edge-case interaction notes.
- Mobile guidance.

3. Copy improvements
